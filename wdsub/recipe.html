Oma's Famous Brownies

  1. Prep Work

    • Preheat the ovan to 350F
    • Rest the butter to room temperature
    • Wash your hands

  2. Mix dry Ingredients

    1. In a large mixing boel add 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 cup of brown sugar, and 1 cup of cocoa powder. Stir throgoughly.
    2. Add 1 tbs of baking powder. Also one tbs of baking soda.
    3. Set aside

  3. Mix wet Ingredients

    1. In a medium mixing bowl, add to large eggs,. Whisk until consistant
    2. Add 1 tbs of vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.
    3. pour a half cup a milk into the wet mix.

  4. Combine Mixtures

    1. Place the dry ingrediants into a stand mixing bowl with the level on low
    2. Pour the wet ingrediants into the mixing bowl
    3. Drop 8 tbs of butter into the mixer (butter is at room temperature). Consistancy should be like oobleck.

  5. Pour and Bake

    1. Grease an 8 by 11 inch backing pan.
    2. Pour mix into the pan. Use spatuala to even it out
    3. Bang it on the table to get rid of the bubbles
    4. Bake for 20 min until desired coonsistancy
    5. Lick the spatualla and bowl.

  6. Bon Apetit...?
